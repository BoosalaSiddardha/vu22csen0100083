GITAM School of Law

GITAM School of Law, ranked among the top 10 law schools in India, focuses on quality legal education and research. The school offers a flexible curriculum tailored to meet the needs of the legal profession.
In NIRF, 2024, GITAM School of Law secured the 37th rank, making it the sole private law institution in AP State to enter the NIRF ranking framework.
GITAM School of Law achieved a ranking of 33 in the Indian Institutional Ranking Framework in 2024, and it currently holds the top position on the IIRF ranking in the AP state.
Career 360 ranks GITAM School of Law in Visakhapatnam as one of the top 10 law schools in India.
