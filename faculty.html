GITAM FACULTY:

GITAM (Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management) faculty comprises highly qualified and experienced professionals dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation.
They are experts in their respective fields, with many holding doctoral degrees and industry experience.
The faculty emphasizes research-oriented learning, holistic development, and interactive teaching methodologies to ensure students are well-prepared for academic and professional challenges.
They play a pivotal role in mentoring students, encouraging participation in research, projects, and extracurricular activities, and contributing to the institution's reputation for academic excellence.
