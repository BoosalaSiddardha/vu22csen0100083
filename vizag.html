Experience Visakhapatnam


Founded in 1980, GITAM's Visakhapatnam campus serves as the main hub of the university, housing the largest number of GITAM institutes in one location.
Nestled between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal, this campus offers an idyllic setting, making it one of the most scenic and serene locations in Andhra Pradesh for higher education.
